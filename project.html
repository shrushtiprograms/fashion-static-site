About Us

This platform is designed to facilitate online shopping and customized stitching services for fashion items. The platform will feature a range of products, including Kurtis, Blouses, Dupattas, and Cholis. It will allow users to purchase ready-made garments, request stitching work, and upload their own designs for custom stitching. Additionally, the platform will enable bulk sellers to connect and manage their orders. The project aims to provide a comprehensive solution for both individual customers and bulk sellers, enhancing the overall shopping and service experience.
